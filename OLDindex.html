Dead Heads



My most fully fleshed out game, a simple 2D top down shooter with working sound, scoreboard, and unlocks.

Eat, Drink, Rave, Repeat


A small game whose title is a reference to a song. Just something quick made as a tech demo of multiple status bars that can be replenished.

Bloons


Featuring only a few monkeys and two maps, one of which is broken, this was a proof of concept of making set courses for objects to follow and auto tracking/firing of projectiles.

Pokemon


An attempt to implement pokemon style battling and movement for an RPG. The battles had some weird bugs with UI and damage numbers. The movement worked well and had functioning animations for travel between areas and the movement itself.

lol2


Angel and Devil


An implementation of the maths problem of Angel vs Devil. The AI was random so made for a boring game.
